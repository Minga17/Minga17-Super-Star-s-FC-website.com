About the Club

Super Stars FC was formed by a core group of players and supporters who share a passion for the game and a goal to push each other to the highest level. Practices are three times weekly; we welcome players who bring commitment and teamwork. We believe in growing together both on and off the field. Our vision is to become a leading soccer club in the region, and play the in high level as competitive games with integrity, known for our competitive spirit, sportsmanship, and community involvement.

FAQ - New Players, Supporters, Volunteers & Parents

Who can join the club?

We welcome players aged 16 and above. We believe, and welcome players of all backgrounds to join our team with a good commitment and teamwork.

When and where are practices held?

Practices are held three times a week, typically on Fridays, Saturdays, and Sundays evenings, but the exact times and locations will be communicated to team members via group chat.

What should I bring to practice?

Players should bring appropriate soccer gear, including cleats, shin guards, water bottles, any personal items they may need, and a positive attitude. We recommend arriving a few minutes early to warm up!

What is the club's code of conduct?

All members are expected to uphold the values of respect, integrity, and sportsmanship. We believe in creating a positive environment for everyone involved.

What is the club's policy on player conduct?

We expect all players to conduct themselves in a manner that reflects positively on the club. This includes showing respect to coaches, teammates, opponents, and officials at all times.

How much does it cost to join the club?

We aim to keep our membership fees affordable. There may be a small fee for league registration and equipment, but we strive to make soccer accessible for all. Please contact us for specific pricing information and any available financial assistance options!

How can I support the club?

You can support Super Stars FC by attending matches, volunteering for events, spreading the word about our club, and considering sponsorship opportunities. Every bit of support helps us grow!
